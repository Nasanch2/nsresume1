Here is my makeup Routine!


  1. Make sure to wash, and moisturize first!

  2. Get your favorite primer and make sure it meets your specific skin needs of your skin type.

  3. Add concealer and color corrector on dark spots or blemishes

  4. Get your foundation, I find a brush and a spatula to have the best results.

    • for very dry skin use chanel foundations because they are water based and have hydrayting ingredients

    • for combination skin I recoment the hous labs foundation

    • a great longlasting an natural looking foundation is the Dior line

  5. Use a contour or bronzing product to add color and define the face

  6. Next I do my eyes

  7. Add a blush and highlight that matches the undertones of the eyeshadow

  8. If desired add powder with a puff to make the makeup last longer

  9. Add maskara and let it dry

  10. Shape and define brows

  11. Add a generous ammount of setting spray

  12. Add your favorite lip product

  13. Finish!
